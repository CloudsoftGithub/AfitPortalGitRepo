Welcome to AFIT Cooperative Society!

Our Vision:

To be a Model Cooperative Society that Provide Diversified Financial and other Allied Products and Services that will Uplift the Quality of Life of its Members and its Immediate Community, AFIT in Particular and the NAF in General.

 Our Mission:

A Viable, Responsive and Sustainable Cooperative with a Passion to Uplift its Members Standard of Living through Provision of Affordable Facilities Build on Accountability, Trust, Fairness, Integrity and Transparency.

Our Core Objectives and Purposes


1. To engage in money transfer and bayad center services for cooperative members.

2. To encourage thrift and savings mobilization among the members for capital formation.

3. To create funds in order to grants loans for productive and providential purposes to our members.


4. To provide goods and services and meet other financial requirements of our members.

5. To engage in housing, transportation services, market management, Agri business and other investment options in members best interest.

6. To promote the cooperative as a way of life for improving the social and economic well-being of its members.
